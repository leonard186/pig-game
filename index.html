Game Rules

There are 2 players and each player can roll the dice as many times as he wishes as long as the following situations don't occur:


  • Do not throw a number 1 because you will loose your current score
  • Do not throw a double more then once in a row because you will loose all your scores

Wichever player reaches the target score first is the winner
